CTiS2022 Conference Registration 8th and 9th July 2022 at IISER, Pune

CSpathshala proudly announces the fourth conference on Computational Thinking in Schools (CTiS2022) on 8th and 9th July, 2022 at IISER, Pune in partnership with the Kaveri Group of Institutes - Dr. Kalmadi Shamarao High Schools, Pune and SCTR's PICT ACM Student Chapter (PASC). Owing to the pandemic, the conference will be held in hybrid format with in-person participation encouraged to the extent possible.
